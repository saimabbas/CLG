Negotiated Settlements

A settlement agreement is a resolution reached outside of a formal court trial, typically including details about the monetary compensation, actions, or concessions each party will undertake to resolve the issue. Settlement negotiation usually occurs when a client’s debts are proved to be valid. In such cases, our attorneys pursue the lowest possible settlement on behalf of the client.
