15 U.S.C. § 1640 provides that a creditor who violates the FCBA is liable to the consumer for the following: Twice the amount of any finance charge associated with the billing error with a minimum of $500 and a maximum of $5,000 in statutory damages or a higher amount if an established pattern or practice of FCBA violations can be demonstrated.
